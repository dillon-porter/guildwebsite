About

no idea is a Mythic Cutting-Edge-oriented guild that raids two times a week during Saturday & Sunday mornings to afternoon from 10 AM - 2 PM CST. Established in 2014 around the middle of Siege of Orgrimmar tier. Previously, we've remained the top 2-day guild and weekend guild on Illidan-US up to the Uldir tier in BFA. Since then, we've been raiding on and off however, achieving most Cutting Edge achievements during each raid tier remains a major focus to this day, with the additional goal of increasing our competitiveness against the top weekend and two-day guilds.

Guild Charter:

1. Atmosphere: our main priority is to have a chill and enjoyable raid environment while we focus on achieving cutting-edge. We will not tolerate any obnoxious behavior. Everyone is encouraged to treat others with the utmost respect while being a part of <no idea>. Feedback is always encouraged towards the leadership and the raid group as a whole as long as there's mutual respect both ways.


2. Efficiency: Having high attendance during our raid times is at the top of the list. We will try to aim to have a roster of 25 people to cover absences and to bring high-performing raiders to progression fights. If you feel like this guild isn't for you or if you have to stop raiding please give us a 2-week notice if possible so we can look for a replacement to cover your spot.

You must be okay with sitting out from time to time. If you are sitting out or asked to sit please be available to come in anytime during our raid times. Since we only raid on a short schedule there isn't a lot of time for errors. We ask our raiders to show up on time for the raid, fully prepared with consumables, addons updated, weak auras downloaded, etc.

We also encourage our raiders to play the class and spec that you're most comfortable within a raid environment, especially on progression. Most importantly, always try to improve your logs each week and bring the best of your ability.


3. Progression: We will not add an optional day for normal or heroic difficulties. We also plan not to save lockouts as soon as we reach the final boss of the raid tier. Our goal is to be efficient as possible on-farm bosses to get our BiS items to help with the last boss. However, there's always a dungeon item or two thats better for a spec. Please find the time before raid and farm the item(s) that you need to help improve your character.

We also expect raiders to come prepared knowing the boss encounters and not go in blind as we have 4 days before we raid to do some research. We'll be using RCLootCouncil and vote on gear for whoever its the biggest upgrade for.

During progression we expect raiders to use consumables when necessary like save a potion or a DPS cooldown for a certain part of a fight if required. We want to see healers pumping out DPS numbers as well when healing requirements are low.

Additonal Guild Info:

  • Check discord daily: This is how we discuss boss strategies and information to our raiders. We expect you to not only read the post but to contribute as often as you can.

  • Post on the #late or absent discord channel: if you have to miss raid: Telling an officer isn’t always adequate.

  • Minimize random AFK’s while we raid: We give a 10minute break halfway throughout the raid day, try your best to save water/bathroom breaks until then. If you do have to AFK please let someone know.

  • Research: Raiders are expected to know the fights along with how to optimize your character. Look at Warcraft logs, Class Discords, and WoWhead.

  • All BoE's that drop in the raid must be traded to the GM or to the guild bank. We will be using Loot council to distribute BoE's that drop to help gear raiders. If a BoE's isn't an upgrade, it will be sold, this helps supply the guild with gold for guild repairs, flask, feast, etc.

  • Trials: The trial period is about 3-4 weekends, or 6-8 raiding days. What we look for is avoidable damage, mechanics, attitude, and attendance.
